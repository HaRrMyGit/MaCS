$f:[a,b] \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$
$f \in R[a,b] \Leftrightarrow S_T(f) - s_T(f) \rightarrow 0$, то есть $\forall \varepsilon > 0 \ \exists \delta > 0 \ \forall T: \lambda_T < \delta \ S_T(f) - s_T(f) < \varepsilon$ 
# Доказательство 
$$
\Rightarrow
$$

$$

$$
$$
I - \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \leq s_T < S_T \leq I + \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \Rightarrow S_T - s_T \leq \frac{2\varepsilon}{3} < \varepsilon
$$
